Online Appointment System for Beauty Salons

Online Appointment System for Beauty Salons is a Web-based Online appointment system for beauty salons for Lingayen, Pangasinan.

The System is exclusive only for the city of Lingayen, Pangasinan. But it is dynamically. There is three login options, Admin, Manager and the Customers. The Admin who runs the whole System can Add and Delete Managers and Salons. The Manager also can register for her/his Salon, can add Staffs and Services. And for the Customers, they are required to register to use the system, they can choose what salons, what services, and choose who are the specific staffs can do the service to book and appointment. The customer also can Rate the service when it's done.

Technology Used

  • UBOLD Bootstrap Framework
  • PHP
  • mySQL
  • Ajax
  • WampServer
  • 000Webhost
